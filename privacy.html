Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

SnapRecall is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app processes your screenshots on your device using Apple's Vision framework for text extraction (OCR). Your images never leave your device.

Data We Collect

SnapRecall does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or registration required
  • No tracking or analytics cookies
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No personal information collected

On-Device Processing

All screenshot scanning, text extraction (OCR), categorization, and search functionality runs entirely on your device. No images or extracted text are sent to any server for these core features.

AI Text Cleanup (Optional Pro Feature)

If you enable the optional AI Text Cleanup feature (available with a Pro subscription), the extracted text only from your screenshots is sent to Google Gemini (operated by Google) for AI-powered cleanup and formatting. Important details:

  • No images or photos are ever sent — only the extracted text content
  • No personal identifiers are included in the request
  • This feature requires your explicit opt-in consent before any data is sent
  • You can disable this feature at any time in Settings
  • Text is processed and not stored by the AI service

Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by Google Gemini: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics

SnapRecall uses TelemetryDeck for privacy-friendly, anonymous usage analytics. TelemetryDeck does not collect any personally identifiable information. No IP addresses, device IDs, or user profiles are created. For more information, see TelemetryDeck's privacy policy.

Data Storage

All app data (screenshots, extracted text, categories, favorites) is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data is removed.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit — for subscription management (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Google Gemini — for optional AI text cleanup (opt-in only, text only)
  • TelemetryDeck — for anonymous, privacy-friendly analytics

Children's Privacy

SnapRecall is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@snaprecallapp.com.
